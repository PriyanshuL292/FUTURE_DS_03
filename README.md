 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to evaluate conversion performance across the business funnel. The focus is on identifying drop-offs between order placement and successful delivery, and uncovering factors affecting conversion efficiency.

---

 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze order-to-delivery conversion rates
* Identify key drop-off points in the funnel
* Compare performance across product categories, states, and payment types
* Provide actionable recommendations to improve conversions

---

 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel

---

 📈 Key Metrics

* Total Customers: 96K
* Total Orders: 99K
* Delivered Orders: 85K
* Customer to Order Conversion: 100% *(dataset limitation)*
* Order to Delivered Conversion: 85.84%
* Customer to Delivered Conversion: 88.13%

---

 ⚠️ Data Limitation

The dataset includes only customers who have already placed orders. Therefore, the top-of-funnel (visitor-to-customer conversion) is not available. The analysis focuses on post-order performance (order → delivery).

---

 🔍 Key Insights

* Approximately 14% of orders fail to reach the delivery stage, indicating a significant drop-off in fulfillment.
* The “Unknown” product category has the lowest delivery rate (~81%), making it the largest contributor to failed deliveries.
* Most major product categories perform consistently (~96–97%), indicating stable operations across core segments.
* Delivery performance is consistent across states, suggesting no major regional bottlenecks.
* Boleto payment method shows slightly lower delivery success compared to credit cards, indicating friction in non-instant payments.

---

 💡 Recommendations

* Investigate and fix product category mapping issues, especially for "Unknown" categories.
* Improve order fulfillment and delivery processes to reduce the ~14% drop-off.
* Encourage faster payment methods such as credit cards to improve success rates.
* Focus optimization efforts on high-volume categories for maximum business impact.
* Monitor and reduce order cancellations and delivery delays.
